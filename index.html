Development duration: 3 months (2021)
Website Link: laftn.com
Description:

Laftn is an online platform that provides voluntary services for not only the Tunisian community but also for tourists and foreign students.
The idea of Laftn is to let people exchange information about their lost or found items (cars, money, paper, animals, phones,) in a secure and organized way. The concept is very simple but also very powerful and useful: if someone loses something he can just share that on Laftn, he can describe what he lost, add pictures, location, and any pieces of information that can help identify his loss. On the other side, whenever someone else finds something, he can do the same (Share, describe, …). The Laftn algorithm will then detect and match each lost item with each find and notify both users in case of a match.
Laftn also provides blood donation services where people can offer to donate their blood or ask for blood donations. The website organizes all the matching based on the user’s location and notifies them by email.
I feel honored to direct and develop this project, which was a great opportunity for me to develop my management and technical skills.
